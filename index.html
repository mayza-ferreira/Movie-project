I love Studio Ghibli movies

Especially Kiki's Delivery Service

"Kiki's Delivery Service" is about a 13-year-old witch who leaves home to begin her solo training in the trade of witchcraft, is an enchanting and inspiring tale that teaches the lesson that you can do anything if you believe in yourself. Young Kiki takes to the sky atop her broom and says goodbye to her friends and family as she must search for a new town where she can settle and learn how to be a witch on her own. A tradition amongst all witches that signifies their passage into adulthood. She lands in a bustling city with her best friend Jiji (her black cat, of course) and starts her new life, but is it as easy as she thinks it is?

Based on the children's fantasy novel "Kiki's Delivery Service" by Eiko Kadono (first published January 25, 1985 by Fukuinkan Shoten).
